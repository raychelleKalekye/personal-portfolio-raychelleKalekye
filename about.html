About Me

My name is Kalekye Raychelle, and I come from Mombasa, Kenya. I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Informatics, where I specialize in artificial intelligence, distributed systems, and data science. My academic journey has given me a strong foundation in both theory and practical application of modern technologies.

I have developed skills in programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, and Java. I also work with frameworks and libraries like React, Flask, and TensorFlow. Additionally, I have hands-on experience in databases (MySQL, MongoDB), cloud services, and version control systems like Git and GitHub.

Beyond technology, I am passionate about problem-solving, research, and building solutions that have a real-world impact. In my free time, I enjoy reading, exploring new tech tools, and engaging in creative design. My long-term career goal is to become a leading AI researcher and innovator, contributing to advancements that improve access to knowledge and opportunities.
